## User Personas

Needs consistent customer handling and policy compliance.

### Operations Manager

Wants repeatable workflows executed reliably.

### AI Platform Engineer

## User Pain Points

- Knowledge scattered across systems
- Inconsistent employee decisions
- Difficult AI onboarding
- Hidden institutional knowledge
- Manual workflow documentation

## Why Now

Organizations are rapidly adopting AI agents, but most business knowledge remains unstructured. Converting existing operational knowledge into executable skills helps bridge this gap safely and efficiently.
